BMI Calculator

The BMI Calculator is a useful tool for assessing an individual's Body Mass Index, which is a measurement of body fat based on height and weight. It helps determine whether a person's weight falls within a healthy range for their height, indicating potential risks associated with underweight, normal weight, overweight, or obesity. BMI Calculator serves as a valuable resource for maintaining overall health.
